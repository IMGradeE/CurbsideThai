This Website!

Eventually this website will implement all the techniques and concepts taught in this course, including CSS, JS, HTML, Graphic Design, and more!

My favorite part of this project is that it will evolve over the course of the semester to reflect my journey through the material. I plan to include a gallery of each version of the site that I publish over the course of the semester to visually represent the evolution of my web design skills.

Comparison Pages & Schedule

In this challenge I built 3 webpages, 2 of which vary only in style and layout, and the other being my tentative weekly schedule. I built the webpages entirely from the ground up, created a condensed version of one of the fonts for a heading, and used GIMP to create list item bullets, background images, and sample colors.

This project was a bit exhausting to flesh out the way that I wanted. There was a tremendous amount of trial and error involved with making everything fit together the way that I wanted and getting the aesthetic I was going for. I do think it turned out well despite the limitations that pure css & html impose on a website though.
